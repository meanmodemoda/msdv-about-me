Srta. Mix-A-Lot

I’m a creative generalist and master of none. I’ve learned to let my heart take me to places, endeavor while enjoying the journey. My life is a trail of pivoting starting from my move across the Pacific in 2012. I chased fashion for many years and eventually dusted off its shimmer and fluff, plunging into the sea of data and catching the beaconing of data visualization. I wish to bring whimsy and style to data, and seek meaning and intentionality in fashion.
